OUR Specials

BURGER

Burgers are known for their versatility, as they can be customized
according to personal preferences and regional variations. They are
commonly served in fast-food restaurants, diners, and pubs, and are
enjoyed by people of all ages around the world.

CHEPALA PULUSU

"Chepala Pulusu" is a traditional Andhra fish curry known for its
rich and tangy flavor. The dish is typically prepared with fresh
fish, usually a variety that is locally available and commonly used
in coastal regions. The fish is cooked in a flavorful and spicy
gravy made with tamarind, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and a blend of
aromatic spices.

BRIYANI's

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in the
Indian subcontinent. It is made with long-grain Basmati rice, meat
(such as chicken, mutton, beef, or seafood), and a blend of spices.
The dish is typically layered, with the rice and meat cooked
separately and then combined in layers before being slow-cooked
together.

PIZZA

Pizza, originating from Italy, is a beloved dish enjoyed worldwide.
It consists of a round, flattened dough topped with savory tomato
sauce, melted cheese (usually mozzarella), and various toppings such
as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, olives, and more.

PUTHAREKULU

Putharekulu are thin, translucent, and paper-like sweets made
primarily from rice starch, sugar, ghee (clarified butter), and
sometimes cardamom for flavoring. They are known for their delicate
texture and sweet taste.
